# Product Vision

## Vision

Make every customer promise—from the first product question to long-term service—visible, executable, and trustworthy. 7ice unifies traditionally fragmented sales, field, and finance processes around a shared lifecycle record.

## Users and jobs

| User                      | Core job                                                    |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| Prospect/customer         | Understand options, request service, and follow commitments |
| Sales advisor             | Qualify demand and create accurate, viable proposals        |
| Scheduler/operations lead | Allocate skilled capacity and manage exceptions             |
| Technician                | Complete safe, evidenced work in the field                  |
| Finance specialist        | Invoice and reconcile contractual events                    |
| Tenant administrator      | Configure access, content, and local policies               |

## Experience promise

The product must be comprehensible in context, progressive rather than overwhelming, and resilient to incomplete information. A portal update should answer “what happens next?”; an internal queue should expose “what needs attention?” without hiding ownership.

## Strategic pillars

1. One lifecycle, many channels: CRM, portals, CMS, and admin use consistent domain states.
2. Configurable control: tenant-level policy and content, with safe platform guardrails.
3. Operational truth: field evidence and financial events are captured once and reused.
4. Trusted by design: clear consent, least privilege, accessible experiences, and auditability.

The vision is implemented incrementally through the [Roadmap](./02_ROADMAP.md), bounded by [Architecture](./03_ARCHITECTURE.md) and [Product Principles](./38_PRODUCT_PRINCIPLES.md).
